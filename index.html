Hello, I am Selina Meyer...

PhD Student at the Natural Language Understanding Lab | University of Technology Nuremberg, Germany.

Welcome to my space on the internet. I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the University fo Technology Nuremberg. I have recently completed my PhD in Information Science at the University of Regensburg, funded through a doctoral scholarship from the German Academic Scholarship Foundation. I love learning new things, reading and spending time in nature.

My PhD research focused on conversational agents for behaviour change. I like to take an interdisciplinary approach to my research and care about ethical implications of technological processes. As part of my studies, I was a visiting researcher at CiTIUS in Santiago de Compostela, Spain in Fall 2022.

I wrote my master's thesis in Information Science on the quality of weight loss information on the German web in November 2019. During my bachelor's I studied British Studies as my major and Information Science as a minor. Take a look at my CV to find out more.
